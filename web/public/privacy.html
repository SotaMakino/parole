Privacy Policy

Effective 22 July 2026

Le Cinque is a free language-learning game run by an individual.

What we store

  • Your username and a one-way bcrypt hash of your password (never the password itself).
  • Your game history (words shown, guesses, learned count).
  • Two strictly-necessary cookies: session (login, ~24h) and player (anonymous guest id, ~1yr). No analytics or tracking cookies.

Third parties

When you tap 🔊, the game word (not personal data) is sent to Google Cloud Text-to-Speech. The site is hosted on Cloudflare and Render, which may process technical data such as your IP to serve requests.
